Sound engineering is my first language, from pre-production and recording to audio editing, mixing and mastering to all things music industry related... and all completely mobile. I aim to keep everything as creative as possible, to work around you, so you can keep the creative flow going in the environments you feel most at home in. The studio that comes to you.

CAL OWEN SOUND

I'm Cal. A professional sound engineer and musician, originaly from the UK. After studying Music Technology at college, I went on to University – where I studied Studio Recording Techniques, Mixing, ADR, Foley, Music For Film, Experimental Sound and more – I graduated with a Honours Degree in Music Technology and moved to London, UK in 2011 where I spent 5 years working at a North London recording studio workign with artists and clients such as Gary Barlow, Joss Stone, ESSO, Fantastic Fireworks, Shayne Ward, Jason Donavan, Bill Kenwright Limited and Ricky Wilson to name a few. In August 2012 I moved to Vancouver and have been freelancing since.
